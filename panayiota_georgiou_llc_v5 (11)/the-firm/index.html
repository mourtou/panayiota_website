The Firm

Panayiota Georgiou LLC is a professional law firm based in Nicosia, Cyprus, offering focused legal support across a broad range of practice areas.

The firm provides targeted legal advice to individuals, families and businesses with an emphasis on confidentiality, careful analysis and practical judgment. Each client relationship is built on trust, transparency and a shared commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

Our areas of practice include Corporate Law, AML/Compliance, Family Law, Immigration Law, International Trade Law, Commercial Law, Contract Law, Trademarks, Trust Law, Intellectual Property, Mergers & Acquisitions, and Legal Transactions.

Our Approach

Every matter is approached with thorough preparation, discretion and a clear understanding of the client's objectives. We believe that great legal work is built on listening carefully, thinking precisely, and communicating clearly.
