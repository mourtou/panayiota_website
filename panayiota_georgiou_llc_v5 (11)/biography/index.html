BIOGRAPHY

Panayiota Georgiou

Founder and Advocate at Panayiota Georgiou LLC, Panayiota brings over a decade of experience across corporate law, compliance, family law, and international trade.

Admitted to the Cyprus Bar and a member of CYBAR, she holds both an LL.B. and an LL.M. in International Trade Law. She is also a certified Compliance Officer (ICA) and Registered Family Law Mediator.

Professional Background

Prior to founding her own firm, Panayiota served in senior roles at leading Cyprus law firms, building expertise in AML/Compliance, corporate transactions, immigration and commercial law. Her career reflects a commitment to rigorous legal practice and client-focused service.
